My Story

Born curious,
built from scratch.

I've been drawn to computers since I was a kid — teaching myself Photoshop and building a soccer website long before I knew what a career in tech looked like.

My career began in product marketing at a global company, taking me across dozens of countries and giving me a wider lens on how people, markets, and products interact. But I never stopped thinking about building things. Eventually, that curiosity led me to Canada — where I started over, studying design and development from scratch.

Today, I bring three perspectives to every product I work on: a marketer's instinct for defining the right problem, a designer's eye for shaping the right experience, and an engineer's discipline for building and improving it with care.

Outside of work, I run marathons, play soccer and golf, camp with my family, and travel whenever I get the chance. I've found that the more I wear myself out, the more I appreciate just being home.
