My Story

Born curious,
built from scratch.

I've been drawn to computers since I was a kid — teaching myself Photoshop and building a football website long before I knew what a career in tech looked like.

I started professionally as a product marketer at a global company, which took me across dozens of countries and gave me a wider lens on how people, markets, and products interact. But I never stopped thinking about building things. Eventually, I made the move to Canada and started over — studying design and development from scratch.

Today, I bring three perspectives to every product I work on: a marketer's instinct for defining the right problem, a designer's eye for shaping the right experience, and an engineer's discipline for building and improving it with care.

Outside of work, I'm quiet but active. I run marathons, play football and golf, go camping with my family, and recharge through travel whenever I can.
