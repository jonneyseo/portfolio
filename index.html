About
01

I don't just build features —
I build products.

Most people pick a lane — marketer, designer, or engineer. I've spent 12 years deliberately refusing to. Not out of indecision, but because the best digital products are built by people who understand all three.

At LG Electronics, I shipped AI TV experiences to 3,000 stores across 15 countries. At SplitMango, I designed and built web products for local businesses from a blank canvas. At PayShepherd, I engineered a B2B SaaS platform from its MVP foundations all the way through architectural modernization — and built an AI-powered invoice analysis tool that saved $80K+ in its first quarter.

My edge isn't that I'm the best marketer, the best designer, or the best engineer. It's that I'm all three — and I know how to move fluently between them to ship things that actually work.
